GDSC are community groups for university students interested in Google developer technologies. By joining GDSC, students grow their knowledge and build solutions.

  • Connect:

    Meet students interested in developer technologies at your university.

  • Learn:

    Learn about a range of technical topics, new skills through hands on workshops, talks and events.

  • Grow:

    Apply new learnings to build great solutions for all local problems.Give back to your community by helping others learn too.
